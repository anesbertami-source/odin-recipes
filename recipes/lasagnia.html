Lasagnia

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made by layering pasta sheets with rich meat sauce,
creamy béchamel or ricotta, and melted cheese. It is baked until golden and
bubbly, creating a warm, flavorful meal with tender layers and a rich, savory taste.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Cook the ground beef with onion and garlic until browned.
  4. Add the tomato sauce and oregano, then simmer for a few minutes.
  5. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  6. Add a layer of lasagna noodles.
  7. Spread ricotta cheese, then sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  8. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used.
  9. Bake for 35–45 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  10. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
